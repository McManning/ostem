About Us

oSTEM propels the gay, lesbian, bisexual, transgender and straight ally community forward with innovative opportunities for students in STEM fields.

We aim to provide support for gay, lesbian, bisexual, transgender and straight ally students by educating peers, organizing social events and connecting with professionals in industry.
